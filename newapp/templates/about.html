About Us

Collate AI is an AI Startup, That uses AI for Business Management and Decision Making In Retail To Generate Financial Reports, co-founded by Martin Sichibeya and Allan Mapumba, An expert Accountant with over 10 years of experience.
Throughout the history of Business, humans have always wished to know what the sales performance for their business will look like. Humans have always wished to predict tomorrow so that they can plan about the quantity of products to purchase to reduce Inventories at their stores. In the new paradigm, AI models will help predict our future sales to help meet purchase Demands, Reduce Inventories and Optimize Profit.
Advancements In Artificial Intelligence or AI promise to fundamentally help us predict the future sales for Retail and wholesale businesses on weekly basis, Monthly basis, Quarterly basis and Yearly basis to help generate powerful financial reports with the help of our expert accountants. This will allow human expert accountants to collaborate with AI models to genearate the reports for retail and wholesale businesses. Over time, these AI model capabilities will revolutionize what it means to generate Financial Business Reports.
